About Us

من نحن

Nutella Bar is Khartoum's best foundation serving Nutella IceCream. It's owned, managed and maintained by a group of wonderful sudanese whose job and number one goal is to make you satisfied.
we offer you a variety of products covered in Nutella chocolate. and we don't just stop at that we offer a large collection of toppings that you can add to your IceCream to make it even better for example you can add Oreo, Kitkat, Twix, kinder bueno and much more.
This is beside our regular IceCream which you can also add topings to, you can even add Biscuits "I mean..... Hell Yeah!" and we also sell Chocolate Bars
Our branches are spread all over the capital we are at Khartoum, Khartoum North, Omdurman and even kalakla so we are always close to you.
our employees are hand picked they are some of the best in the business in our country they will treat you will and give you your order in the best shape it can ever be, because they always know that your hapiness is our hapiness.

نوتيلا بار هو أفضل مؤسسة في الخرطوم تقدم آيسكريم النوتيلا. المحل مملوكة ومدار من قبل مجموعة من الشباب الرائعين و هدفهم الأول هو إرضاءكم

هذا بجانب الآيس كريم العادي الذي يمكنك أيضًا إضافة طبقة إليه ، حتى يمكنك إضافة البسكويت "أعني ..... وأخيرا !" ونقوم أيضًا ببيع ألواح الشوكولاتة
تنتشر فروعنا في جميع أنحاء العاصمة فنحن في الخرطوم والخرطوم بحري وأمدرمان وحتى الكلاكلة لذلك نحن دائما قريبون منك
